Books

This page just has some of the books I have read recently. I decided to put this page up after conquering the Throne of Glass series by Sarah J. Maas, which is as captivating as it is long and I recommend to everyone who’s looking to get into fiction!

Book Author Rating Thoughts
There Are Places in the World Where Rules Are Less Important Than Kindness Carlo Rovelli 4/5 A fun writing style, where Rovelli seems to appreciate the fly-trap nature of his observations.
Remarkably Bright Creatures Shelby Van Pelt 5/5 My definition of a comfort read. A gripping story that makes you want to hug your loved ones and appreciate the important things in life.
Kingdom of Ash Sarah J Maas 5/5 A perfect ending to an epic series.
The Call of the Wild Jack London 4/5 A perfect story for a cold winter day. A good reminder that there is some untamed nature in all of us.
The Love Hypothesis Ali Hazelwood 4/5 A fun love story that makes you remember that love can happen anywhere, any time.
The Mountain in the Sea Ray Nayler 5/5 A masterpiece. Nayler makes the story feel totally real despite its ridiculousness. A perfect intertwining of AI, philosophy, and consciousness.
Why Fish Don’t Exist Lulu Miller 4.5/5 This book may or may not change your life (in a good way).
Going Infinite: The Rise and Fall of a New Tycoon Michael Lewis 2/5 Wasn’t impressed. Lewis seems to have some sort of agenda while writing this book, and I got rubbed the wrong way.
The Age of AI and OUr Human Future Henry Kissinger 3.5/5 Useful and informative, but not groundbreaking.
The Joy of X Steven Strogatz 3/5 This book is great if you want to read about math without doing any.
Queen of Shadows Sarah J Maas 5/5 Maybe the best book in the series.
The Assassin’s Blade Sarah J Maas 4.5/5 The short story format keeps you on the edge of your seat. Sam.
Daisy Jones & The Six Taylor Jenkins Reid 4.5/5 This reads like the characters are right there with you. A fun and flashy story that you will want to finish in one sitting
Heir of Fire Sarah J Maas 5/5 What a journey this one is. Contains some of the most memorable moments in the whole series.
What I Talk About When I Talk About Running Haruki Muarakami 3.5/5 I started running more after reading this, not sure why though.
Crown of Midnight Sarah J Maas 4.5/5 Great character development. Things are just getting started!
Throne of Glass Sarah J Maas 4.5/5 You’ve got to start somewhere, and this story has some essential moments and serves as the foundation for the series.
AI For Good Juan Ferres and William Weeks 4.5/5 An essential, accessible read for those that want to learn about the bright side of AI.
